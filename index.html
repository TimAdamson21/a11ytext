Improving The Accessibility Of Course Materials

  • Problem: Inaccessible Course Material

    When a professor uploads an image of text, whether it's a page of a book or their handwritten notes, it is not accessible to those who are visually impaired.

  • Current Solutions

    Currently, at the University of Washington, students with visual impairements often come to UW staff with academic materials on which they can't use text-to-speech, and these staff members type up the documents by hand for each individual student.

  • Enter A11y Text

    With A11y Text, these students can upload all of their academic materials as PDFs, JPGs, or PNGs and download versions they can use in a simple text file.
